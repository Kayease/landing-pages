Our Story

Founded in 1974, VELOX has been at the forefront of athletic footwear innovation for over 50 years. What started as a small company in Oregon has grown into a global leader in sports technology and design.

Our mission has always been clear: to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world. We believe that if you have a body, you are an athlete. This philosophy drives everything we do.
